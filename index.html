about me

“I began volunteering to design web pages at my day marketing job as well as taking online courses in Ruby and JavaScript. When listening to a TED talk about careers, the lecturer offered the vital advice: to think about the activity or task where you don’t realise time passing and make this your career. It was this advice that has taken me on this journey into web development. I have enjoyed every moment of learning so far, and I am eager to start a position as a full stack developer.”
